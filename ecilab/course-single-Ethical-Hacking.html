Certification

Certifications are special documents (certificates) that you can receive from accredited institutions or certifying boards. These documents show proof of your competency, certain professional level or proficiency in a specific skill. To earn a certification, most professionals pay application fees and meet certain requirements to take related certification exams. After completion of certifying exams with passing scores, professionals become eligible to receive the related certification. Because many certifications can be challenging and time-consuming to pursue, certified professionals are often more qualified for competitive roles. Possessing a certification can also improve your reputation in your career industry.
